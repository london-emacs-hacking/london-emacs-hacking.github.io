4.1.3 Xanadu

I find myself writing more and more stuff in org. With that comes the need of having a way to make and show the interconnections between those writings. In other words I'm finding myslef in need of a more powerful reference mechanism. Every now and then when I'm looking for answers on-line or try to sketch some solutions myself I'm going back to Ted Nelson and Xanadu and keep thinking that I'm just trying to re-implement some of his ideas in Emacs. When I've realised that and started to look around myself I found that some ppl already did some of that. Nevertheless I think that this would make for a great elisp exercise and I would love to see how far we can go. I believe some of the results may prove to be practical (I know I want some of that) but I want to do it mainly for the fun of it! PS Other source of inspiration could be some of the wiki systems
